Production & process

After deciding on the concept,I made a rough sketch in Photoshop to base the composition on .The hunt for photos of hand began,and after collecting quite a few cut them out and placed then into the composition.The fire was created mainly by warping pictures of flames into the shapes I desired .A 3D heart shtock by shoofly bg shoolfly-stock was brought in and maniputated to create the heart .I used Apophysis to creare abstract renders which I used to create motion and texture in the backguound.Piximator was used to create some final touches,which was brought back into phtoshop for finsl composite.
